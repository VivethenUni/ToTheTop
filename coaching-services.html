What To Expect From Our Coaches

Just like anyone, our expert coaches were once total rookies themselves looking to improve. Our large variety of coaching services were created to help you learn the various aspects of playing like a pro.

Our Services

Service Price Description
1v1 A Pro $25/hr Looking for a classic playing session? A one on one verses a pro may be what you need to step up your game. Our coaches will open their mind to you while playing and explain how they decided what moves to make and how to handle specific interactions that take place during your games.
Preparing For A Match $22/hr A highly overlooked part of going pro is preparing for an upcoming match. Whether it is understanding a game's meta, creating a team for an upcoming tournament, or preparing for an unusual matchup, our coaches will guide you through the best ways to preemptively prepare for anything that comes your way.
VOD Review $18/hr Reviewing your past games can help coaches identify your strengths and weaknesses. After understanding these weaknesses, such as selecting repetitive options, or panicking under disadvantageous situations, your coach will be able to provide guidance to help overcome these weaknesses in your future games.
